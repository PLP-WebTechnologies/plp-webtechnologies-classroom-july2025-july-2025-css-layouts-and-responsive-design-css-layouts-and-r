About Grandeur Diner

Nestled in the heart of Lekki, Grandeur Diner offers an unparalleled dining experience that celebrates the world's most sought-after cuisines alongside Nigeria's rich culinary heritage.

Our master chefs combine international techniques with locally-sourced ingredients to create dishes that delight both the adventurous foodie and the traditional palate.

From our elegant dining room to our intimate private spaces, every detail is crafted to provide a memorable experience for our distinguished guests.
